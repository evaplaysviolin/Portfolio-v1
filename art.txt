      <div class="art artDescription">
        <p><b>ASSORTED CALLIGRAPHY & LETTERING</b></p>
        <p><b>Dates Completed:</b> 01.19.2014, 06.08.2016, 07.22.2016</p>
        <p><b>Description:</b> Debussy quote from four years ago during initial interest and trials with calligraphy, 
          Planet Mnemonic from two years ago during experiments with watercolor pencils, 
          and Harry Potter quote also from two years ago.</p>
        <p><b>Materials:</b> Paper<br>
          Various calligraphy nibs and holder<br>
          Calligraphy ink<br>
          Watercolor pencils<br>
          Uni-ball Signo 0.8mm in gold</p>
      </div>
      
      <div class="art artDescription">
        <p><b>FAKE FLOWERS</b></p>
        <p><b>Dates Completed:</b> 09.14 & 20.2017</p>
        <p><b>Description:</b> Rolled felt flowers + tissue paper for a photobooth backdrop assembled on a sheet. 
          Rolled coffee filter flowers (dyed with diluted acrylic paint) for floral wreath centerpieces, 
          the wreath forms being dissected pool noodles wrapped in ribbon. 
          Both projects requiring an unholy amount of hot glue. So much glue.</p>
        <p><b>Materials:</b> Flat flannel sheet<br>
          Yards of craft felt<br>
          Tissue paper<br>
          Coffee filters<br>
          Acrylic paint<br>
          Painter’s tape<br>
          Pool noodles<br>
          Wide polyester ribbon<br>
          An infinite number of glue sticks and a glue gun</p>
      </div>
      
      <div class="art artDescription">
        <p><b>WEDDING INVITATIONS</b></p>
        <p><b>Date Completed:</b> 05.07.2017</p>
        <p><b>Description:</b> Wedding invitation that was mailed out to all invitees, hand-drawn in Photoshop (including lettering, excluding Chinese characters). 
          Background inspired by stained glass windows. Printed on 8.5” x 11” paper.</p>
        <p><b>Materials:</b> Photoshop<br>
          Laser color printer<br>
          80 lb. paper</p>
      </div>
      
      <div class="art artDescription">
        <p><b>OTHER WEDDING STATIONERY</b></p>
        <p><b>Dates Completed:</b> 01.28.2017, 02.24.2017</p>
        <p><b>Description:</b> Save the Dates and Thank You cards drawn in Photoshop, themed to match each other (reusing floral graphics, 
          which were also used on the wedding website). Cut to A1 size. 
          Envelopes for all were hand-lettered and quadruple-checked for mistakes, of which we found two.</p>
        <p><b>Materials:</b> Photoshop<br>
          Laser color printer<br>
          80 lb. paper<br>
          Guillotine paper cutter<br>
          A1 and #10 envelopes<br>
          Metallic gold Sharpies</p>
      </div>
      
      <div class="art artDescription">
        <p><b>ASSORTED COMMISSIONS</b></p>
        <p><b>Dates Completed:</b> 06.09.2017, 09.22.2015</p>
        <p><b>Description:</b> Diner-style menu (11” x 17”) commissioned by a friend for her young daughter to use during mealtimes. 
          T-shirts designed for a child care called My Little Sunshine to create army of brightly-dressed toddlers when out on a field trip. 
          Though very interested in learning how to screen print, printing was done through Custom Ink.</p>
